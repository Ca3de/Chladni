Realistic physics: the plate surface oscillates sinusoidally. When the plate's vertical acceleration exceeds gravity, particles launch into the air. While airborne they follow ballistic arcs. On landing, friction slides them toward the nodal lines. Use the tone generator or your microphone to drive the plate vibration.
